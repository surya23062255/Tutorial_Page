What is Computer ??

Computer is an electronic data processing device, which accept and store data input and generate the output in a required format. The word "Computer" is extended form of the word "Compute", i.e., to Calculate. It can perform the arthemetic operations at fast speed.

Types of Computer

  1. Analog Computer
  2. Digital Computer
  3. Hybrid Computer

Analog Computer

In analog computers, constant quantities are used. Calculations are done with physical quantities like voltage, length, current etc. Instruments measuring such quantities are analog instruments like voltmeter, ammeter. The analog computer operates by measuring rather than counting. The main advantage is that all the calculations happen in parallel and hence, they are fast. Analog computers are mostly used in medical science.

Digital Computer

Digital computers work on discrete data. They convert the data into digits (binary digits) and all operations are performed on these digital at extremely fast rate. A digital computer basically knows how to count the digits and add up the digits. Digital computers are much faster and far more accurate than analog computers. Computers used for business and scientific applications are digital computers.

The Digital Computers are classified into four categories.

Micro Computer

A microcomputer is one whose CPU is a microprocessor. These are typically single-microprocessor, single user-systems designed to perform basic operations such as educational, training, small business applications, etc. They are mainly used for offices, homes, schools, etc. Microcomputer is a complete computer on a smaller scale and is usually a synonym for the more general term 'personal computer'.

Mini Computer

Minicomputer also called mid-range server, a term no longer used much, is a computer of a size intermediate between a microcomputer and a mainframe computer. Typically minicomputers are stand-alone computers sold to small and medium-sized businesses for general business applications and large enterprises for department-level operations. In recent years minicomputers have evolved into 'mid-range servers' and are part of a network.

Mainframe Computer

Mainframe computer is an industry term for a large computer. It is manufactured by a big company like IBM. A mainframe computer is large but not as large as a supercomputer and has more processing power than some other classes of computers, such as minicomputers and personal computers. Most large-scale computer-system architectures were established in the 1960s. Mainframe computers are often used as servers.

Super Computer

Supercomputers are the most powerful computers as compared to other computers. Many processors run simultaneously in these, making them extremely fast and powerful. These computers are capable of handling large amounts of computation that are beyond human capability. They perform billions of instructions per second.

Note :-

  • India's first Super Computer is PARAM-10000
  • World's first Super Computer is CRAY-1

Hybrid Computer

Hybrid computers utilize the best qualities of both digital and analog computers. Some of these calculations are done in an analog way and the rest of them are done in a digital way. Hybrid computers are used in weather forecasting.

Characteristics of Computer

Speed

Computer is a very fast device. It can calculate data at very high speed. Computer speed is measured in terms of microseconds, nanoseconds, picoseconds, etc. For example, a microcomputer can execute millions of instructions per second, but a supercomputer executes instructions millions of times faster than a microcomputer.

Storage Capacity

Computers can store large amounts of data and information for a very long time. It can store any type of data and information. Its memory system has two types of memory as main memory and auxiliary memory. Auxiliary storage devices are secondary storage devices of a computer system.

Accuracy

Computer system is a highly consistent machine. Its accuracy is much higher than that of humans. It can calculate at almost 100% accuracy. All the operations are to be conducted by a computer with the same accuracy. Errors can occur in a computer system, but only because of incorrect human input data or incorrect programming code.

Versatility

Computer systems are very versatile machines. The most amazing feature of a computer system is that it can perform a variety of different activities, from simple calculations to complex scientific operations and is also capable of preparing the examination marksheets, bills etc. They can also communicate with each other to send and receive data. In short, a computer is capable of performing any task.

Diligence

Diligence means free from monotony, exhaustion, lack of concentration. Due to this quality, the computer can work for many hours without any errors. Human beings have feelings like sadness, depression, carelessness etc. These feelings and weakness affect the work done by man. A computer can perform the same tasks again and again with the same accuracy because there is no human weakness in the computer.

Automation

For a particular task, the computer does not depend on the user, it automatically forwards the tasks in the forward direction till its completion. Computers can be easily programmed to perform a range of tasks as per the requirements. The computer automatically executes these instructions sequentially.
